Zhukovskiy Sights

Zhukovskiy is a great town with plenty of interesting places to visit. If you are new to the town and you want to find some sights where you can spend your free time, but not waste it, then this leaflet will be extremely useful for you.

Zhukovskiy Municipal Museum

In this museum you can learn about history of the town and history of the Russian aviation. Here you can see various interactive exposition, for example “History of sky’s conquest”. This exposition tells us colorfully about aviation history since XX century to present days, showing lots of aviators’ equipment, like helmets and flight suits. You also can literally experience piloting the plane with training apparatuses, which are used to teach actual pilots.

Music and Drama Theater by A. Aysin


Created in 1991, this theater offers massive variety of plays to a viewer. For all of readers who like to spend their free time in a useful way, this place can provide pearls of the world literature, such as famous “Pygmalion” by B. Shaw. Nevertheless, you also can find here an entertainment for children of any age, such as “The Wizard of Oz”.

“Pyatiy Dom” (“The 5th House”) Art Gallery


For those of you who loves art of painting, we can advise visiting “The 5th House” art gallery. If you are looking for some thematical expositions to visit, this gallery can provide massive diversity of ones, for example “Zhukovskiy’s ceramists” ceramics exposition. You can also take here some art lessons, no matter how old are you. After finishing your course, your works have chance to be a part of students' art expositions.
